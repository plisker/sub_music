Music

I'm very passionate about music: I can often be found experimenting with an instrument or searching for and listening to new music, and I recently built my own electric guitar! For insight into my eclectic and evolving taste, you can check out my Last.fm listening history or some Spotify playlists I've created.

Over the past few years, I've developed a passion for recording and all things sound engineering. I've recorded some covers with my sisters, Claire and Karyn, and have started hosting and recording concerts for friends. Hope you enjoy my recordings—check them out below!
